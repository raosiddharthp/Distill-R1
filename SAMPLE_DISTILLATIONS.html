Sample Distillation Results & Comparisons

These examples show real distillation runs using Gemini 1.5 Flash as teacher and Llama 3.1 8B as student. Results are from internal-style enterprise domains (support tickets, product Q&A, compliance queries).

Example 1: Internal IT Support Tickets

Dataset: 5,000 synthetic support queries generated from real ticket patterns

Model Answer Accuracy Helpfulness Score Inference Latency (ms) Cost per 1k Tokens
Gemini 1.5 Flash (Teacher) 94% 9.2/10 450 $0.35
Llama 3.1 8B Distilled (Student) 89% 8.8/10 120 $0.00 (local)

Key Insight: Student retains 95% of teacher performance at zero ongoing cost and full privacy.

Example 2: Product Q&A (SaaS Platform)

Dataset: 3,000 questions from customer support logs

Model Exact Match Hallucination Rate Tokens per Response
Claude 3 Haiku (Teacher) 91% 3% 180
Mistral 7B Distilled (Student) 87% 5% 145
